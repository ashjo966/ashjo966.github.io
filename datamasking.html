GDPR Compliant Datamasking tool

Business Analysis GDPR Investment Banking ETL B2B
June 2017 – December 2019

Tools Used:

JIRA Confluence MS Excel

Problem Statements

  • The implementation of EU GDPR regulations exposed Morgan Stanley to substantial compliance risks when migrating customer data from Production to lower environments (Dev/QA) for testing.
  • Legacy data transfer systems lacked user interfaces and automated masking capabilities, forcing engineering teams to manually sanitize Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and Material Non-Public Information (MNPI).
  • Manual sanitization requirements frequently resulted in data exposure, as developers occasionally bypassed tedious protocols and utilized raw, sensitive customer data for testing.
  • These manual sanitizations resulted in sluggish release pipelines and high potential for compliance breaches.

Discovery

  • Collaborated with legal, compliance, and data privacy stakeholders to map out a product roadmap.
  • Interviewed application owners and software engineering teams to evaluate the exact characteristics of test data needed for development cycles, and features required for a data transfer & data masking tool.

Solution

  • Spearheaded the conceptualization and delivery of "Alcazar," a fully GDPR-compliant data masking and synthetic data generation platform.
  • Engineered the platform to autonomously scan, identify, and categorize sensitive PII and MNPI data elements.
  • Empowered developer teams to automatically mask production-grade data in real-time, instantly generating compliant, high-fidelity synthetic datasets for sandboxed testing.

Implementation Process

  • Worked as a Business Analyst to translate the data transfer tool features into clear engineering tasks.
  • Wrote user stories, groomed them with the team, and participated in all product demos.
  • Managed sprint execution, daily standups, and stakeholder demo loops to ensure high-velocity delivery.

Results

91% Data quality satisfaction

91% of application owners confirmed that the test data post masking met or exceeded expectations within the first 60 days post-launch.

78% User Approval Score

The upgraded compliance tool earned a 78% positive response rating from internal users in a survey collected within the first 60 days post-launch.
