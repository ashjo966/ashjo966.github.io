Card controls - mobile banking app

Product Discovery UX design iOS Android Mobile Banking B2C
January 2020 – March 2022

Tools Used:

JIRA Confluence Adobe XD Figma MS Visio MS Excel

Problem Statements

  • HSBC's retail banking customers lacked on-the-go, self-service security features to manage their debit and credit cards directly within the HSBC mobile banking application.
  • The absence of modern card controls forced users to rely on customer support for routine requests, driving up call center volumes and operational costs.
  • This outdated banking experience generated frequent complaints, negatively impacting HSBC’s market position as a customer-centric bank across the world.

Discovery

  • Executed an in-depth analysis of major retail mobile banking applications across leading private banks in India to identify feature gaps and establish industry standards.
  • Conducted structured interviews with customer service executives working in HSBC branches across India, the UK, and the Middle East to pinpoint credit card pain points and banking experience in general.
  • Analyzed and documented historical credit card attrition metrics across Retail and High-Net-Worth Individual (HNI) segments to build a compelling funding case for mobile app.

Solution

  • Partnered with senior product leadership to pitch strategic mobile banking enhancements, successfully securing executive funding for the initiative.
  • Played a pivotal role in defining a comprehensive product roadmap spanning the UK, Middle East, India, Hong Kong, and Singapore markets.
  • Collaborated closely with UX/UI leads to develop high-fidelity, visually compelling prototypes utilized as strategic visual aids during funding pitches.

Implementation

  • Worked as Product Owner for the end-to-end delivery of core card control journeys in the UAE market, including temporary block/unblock, travel plan configurations, and monthly spend limits for both debit and credit cards.
  • Following the successful UAE rollout, spearheaded the development of critical security workflows for the UK market, including "report damaged/stolen" and replacement card ordering features.
  • Managed the product backlog, authored user stories, facilitated engineering refinement sessions, validated sprint deliverables, and actively participated in all agile ceremonies.
  • Orchestrated collaborative sessions with UX/UI designers and engineering architects to map customer journeys and design scalable wireframes.

Results

55% Support Call Reduction

Achieved a 55% reduction in calls to customer care hotlines to report lost or damaged cards within 30 days of launch.

46% Monthly spend limit

46% adoption rate for set a monthly spend limit on cards feature in UAE post 30 days of introduction

18% Temp Block/Unblock

18% adoption rate for temporary block & unblock card feature in UAE post 30 days of introduction

8% Travel plan

8% adoption rate for set a travel plan on your card feature in UAE post 30 days of introduction

+16 Net Promoter Score

Successfully increased the mobile app's dedicated Net Promoter Score (NPS) from -12 to +16.
