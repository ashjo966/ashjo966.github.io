Onboarding as a Service (ObaaS)

Product Discovery Customer Onboarding Platform Investment Banking B2B
April 2024 – August 2025

Tools Used:

JIRA Confluence MS Visio Figma Notion MS Excel

Problem Statements

  • Wells Fargo's Corporate Investment Banking (CIB) and Commercial Banking (CB) onboarding processes were historically manual, fragmented, and siloed across multiple systems, creating operational bottlenecks.
  • Despite sharing nearly identical operational workflows, the CIB and CB divisions maintained completely separate onboarding platforms.
  • Clients experienced lack of visibility into their onboarding status, leading to frequent follow-ups and high operational overhead.
  • The average onboarding completion time for both divisions was 40 days, lagging behind the 7-day industry benchmark set by top competitors (JPMorgan Chase, Bank of America, Citibank).

Discovery

  • Conducted competitor analysis to understand how much time our competitors took to complete an onboarding.
  • Focus interviews with Relationship Managers to capture client expectations and identify key workflow friction points.
  • Analyzed historical onboarding data to pinpoint critical bottlenecks, operational blockers, and primary sources of delay.
  • Evaluated existing internal tooling and integration capabilities to determine the viability of transitioning to a centralized "platform-as-a-service" model.

Solution

  • Led the creation of "Onboarding as a Service" (ObaaS), a unified, modern API-first orchestration layer for Commercial Investment Banking and Commercial Banking divisions.
  • Designed the platform to serve as a shared internal capability, enabling multiple business lines to instantly consume scalable onboarding workflows and compliance checks.
  • Led the creation of core operational capabilities like the onboarding request withdrawal feature to enhance system flexibility.
  • Following the successful rollout of the withdrawal module, led the development of a dynamic Request for Information (RFI) engine to capture KYC data, engineered with the modularity to support future consumer-facing deployment.

Implementation Process

  • Directed the product design and ideation phases for core capabilities, aligning closely with senior business stakeholders to define the capability vision.
  • Actively collaborated with frontline onboarding staff to gather requirements and set eligibility rules.
  • Maintained continuous alignment with other product owners to ensure every other feature accounts for withdrawal scenarios.
  • Managed the product backlog, reviewing and approving user stories and acceptance criteria written by the Business Analyst prior to engineering handover.
