Mortgage SaaS Product

Product Discovery MVP Planning Mortgage Order Management SaaS B2B
April 2022 – March 2024

Tools Used:

Azure DevOps Miro Zeplin MS Excel

Problem Statements

  • ServiceLink was facing a major roadblock in its expansion plans: its 20+ years old legacy mortgage processing tool, that formed the backbone of its operating model, was outdated and becoming increasingly difficult to run.
  • System modifications required for regulatory updates or process improvements resulted in long turnaround times and prohibitive costs.
  • The legacy mortgage processing tool was also hosted on-prem. This made it difficult to scale up as the workforce expanded, leading to frequent outages.
  • Following its acquisition by Fidelity National Finance, a strategic gap was discovered: ServiceLink's 25+ years of proprietary mortgage industry knowledge was trapped within an outdated business model that relied too heavily on transaction commissions rather than scalable revenue streams.

Discovery

  • Spoke to SMEs across the board within ServiceLink to understand the depth of their knowledge of the mortgage process.
  • Conducted competitor analysis to scan the mortgage sector and its outlook.
  • Deep research of ServiceLink's clients - big US banks like JP Morgan Chase, BoA and Citibank - to get an idea of how they envision mortgages will be processed in the future.
  • Focus interviews with ServiceLink CXOs to understand their vision for the organisation's future and its alignment with Fidelity National Finance's acquisition objectives.

Solution

  • Proposed leveraging ServiceLink's 25+ years of institutional knowledge to create "Exos," a modern, cloud-native SaaS mortgage processing platform on Microsoft Azure.
  • Exos will be designed to replace the legacy mortgage processing tool while also serving as a commercial SaaS product for competitors seeking modernization, resulting in a new, recurring revenue stream.
  • Successfully pitched Capgemini as the best partner to take this initiative forward, drawing into our deep understanding of ServiceLink's pain points and long term vision.

Implementation Process

  • Spearheaded the initiative as the Product Manager and Product Owner for one of the platform's core module: Order Management.
  • Applied the MoSCoW matrix and developed the MVP roadmap as a Product Manager for Exos in collaboration with senior stakeholders from ServiceLink.
  • Acted as a Product Owner for my pod, which composed of 1 Microservice Designer, 2 Microservice Developers, 2 UI Developers, 2 QAs and 1 UI designer.
  • Drove the development of critical features for the Exos tool such as Order Creation, Tracking, Cancellation, and Closure.
  • Following the successful rollout of Order Management, transitioned to leading the development of a fully automated invoicing engine, dynamically driven by applicant profiles, property types, and specific client requirements.
  • Facilitated regular project-level product demos, establishing iterative feedback loops to calibrate delivery and ensure alignment with business expectations.

Results

33% Turnaround Time Reduction

Reduced average turnaround times for all orders from origination till disbursement by 33%, from 6 days to 4 days.

+43 Client Net Promoter Score (NPS)

Drove a significant increase in NPS, jumping from +16 to +43 within the first three months of launch.
